Release 1.0 (Soon!)

Get ready for our first big update! In this version of our game, we're adding some cool stuff.

What's Coming:

  • Meet Mortis: Mortis is a character in the game who will give you missions to complete. He's like your guide through the game.
  • Background Music: We're adding music to the game! The first track is called "Mandarin." It's awesome!
  • Torches: You'll be able to carry torches to light up dark areas in the game. It'll make exploring more fun!

What's Not Coming:

We had some ideas for the game that we decided not to include:

  • Inventory System: Originally, we thought about adding a system to keep track of all the items you collect. But we decided it would make the game too complicated.
